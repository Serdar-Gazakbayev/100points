Privacy Policy for 100points

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the application 100points ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when granted access to a TikTok account. This application is designed for data analysis purposes.

2. Information We Collect

100points collects the minimum amount of data necessary for its analytical function. We do not access or store video content files, private messages, or sensitive personal identification numbers.

2.1. Authentication Information

We use a secure access token provided by TikTok's OAuth 2.0 process to authorize API requests. 100points does not collect, see, or store your TikTok password.

2.2. Public Profile Information

We access basic public profile data such as your username, display name, and profile picture to identify your account within the application.

2.3. Performance Metrics

We collect publicly available statistics associated with your account and its content, including follower counts, view counts, likes, shares, and comments, to generate analytical reports.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Securely connect 100points to the official TikTok API.
  • Display performance metrics in the application's analytical dashboard.
  • Analyze content effectiveness to help understand performance trends.
  • Ensure 100points operates in compliance with TikTok's Developer Policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. We implement appropriate technical and administrative security measures to protect the data we handle.

4.2. Access tokens and collected data are stored securely.

4.3. We will never sell your personal information to any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1. 100points interacts directly with the official TikTok API to collect data.

5.2. Our use of the API is in full compliance with TikTok's Privacy Policy and Developer Policies.

6. Data Subject Rights

As the owner of the account whose data is being analyzed, you have the right to:

  • Revoke 100points's access to your data at any time through your TikTok account settings (under "Security and login" -> "Manage app permissions").
  • Request a copy of the data we have collected from your profile.
  • Request the deletion of your data from our systems.

7. Data Retention

7.1. We retain statistical data only as long as it is necessary for our analytical purposes.

7.2. Account owners can request the deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

8.1. We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational or legal reasons.

8.2. The most current version of the policy will always be available at this URL.
